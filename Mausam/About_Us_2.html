About Us


Here at Verdant, the belief is that every dish pays homage to the best of what nature has to offer. We advocate for chance in vegan dining and seek to infuse a lush, fresh and vibrantly delicious plant-based cuisine that is as indulgent as it is nourishing. At Verdant, luxury meets sustainability.
Our Philosophy
Every ingredient is delicately chosen to bring out the rich flavor of the dish whilst also ensuring that the meal supports ethical and environmentally friendly practices at yap; eating green does not have to mean compromising on taste, luxury and creativity. Inspired by the pieces of nature around us, Verdant embraces the concept of a harmony where each exquisite dish is a work of art that nurtures the planet.
Our Culinary Experience
We warmly invite you to take the plunge and enter our vivid world where plant-based dining knows no bounds. From nourishing bowls that burst with color to gourmet entrees that are meticulously plated to perfection; every dish at Verdant is full of passion, innovation, and imagination. Our professional chefs develop very sophisticated yet incredibly hearty dishes by incorporating modern innovation into traditional styles. Each dish, such as the truffle wild mushroom risotto, heirloom tomato carpaccio, and even the rich, dairy-free chocolate fondant, is crafted with immense dedication to premium quality.
Our Menu
Among a multitude of options, we are proud to offer: Artfully Crafted Appetizers: Featuring polenta crisps served with red pepper coulis and zucchini blossoms filled with roasted cashew cheese. Hearty & Nourishing Bowls: Budha bowls saturated with low glycemic grains, roasted vegetables, and dressing that is sure to enhance one’s mood. Signature Mains: Charred eggplant drizzled with Tahini, saffron risotto, and plant based ‘steak’ with peppercorn sauce gives an ultimate flavor wow. Decadent Desserts: Enjoy our signature Avocado lime cheesecake or our luscious pistachio panna cotta made without any dairy. Refreshing Beverages: Enjoy uniquely designed cocktails, press juices, and organic wines that perfectly accent the whole experience at Verdant.
Our Space
At Verdant, it is all about making you feel at home while ensuring you enjoy the beauty of nature. Our space is filled with gentle diffused lighting, exquisite root textures, and dangling plants, all of which creates an environment that enlightens your spirit while making it easy to indulge in good food.
Join Us
At Verdant, irrespective of your dietary choices, we make it a point to introduce you to innovative plant-centered cuisine that will redefine how you view food. Come, relish life, taste the vibrant flavors and embrace eco-friendliness with us. Allow us to transform your perception of nature and gastronomy; few bites at a time.
